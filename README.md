## Branches

|--------|------------|------------------|-------------------------------------|
| `6.x`  | Laravel 6  | Old `make:auth`  | OAuth authentication with Socialite |
| `8.x`  | Laravel 8  | Breeze, Livewire | Service account authentication      |
| `11.x` | Laravel 11 | Breeze, Livewire | Service account authentication      |
